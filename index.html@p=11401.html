School-Based Behavior Consultation

School-based consultation focuses on skill development and challenging behavior reduction for academic success. Kidz Companion, Inc., provides ABA therapy and behavioral support interventions at school and in the home. This can improve social skills, academics, vocational and functional skills, and communication.

Academic Settings

We provide behavior consultative services in public and private school settings to address the challenging behaviors of students with autism spectrum disorder, who often require unique behavior treatments that will be more effective in a school setting once educators understand the treatment plan.

We work collaboratively with school administrators to develop behavior intervention plans and effective systems of reinforcement to help achieve meaningful objectives in the classroom.

In this, we can help educators:

  • In creating individualized ABA behavior treatment plans.
  • In developing ASD+ intervention programs.
  • In providing on-site professional services, including comprehensive assessments.

An important element of behavior consultation is that it shifts the principles of ABA into a broader arena, one focused on behavior that goes beyond ASD or other related disorders.

Special Education

Special education teachers perform many of the same duties as general education teachers, such as planning and coordinating assignments and activities for their students. However, special educators often approach these tasks in different ways. While general education teachers may have a uniform approach for addressing an entire class, special education teachers must create assignments that are aligned with each student’s academic abilities.

ABA therapists develop research-based behavior intervention plans to reduce problem behaviors. We also collaborate with educators to develop individualized education programs that detail customized instructional approaches to accommodate the specific student’s needs.

Our ABA-trained professionals can equip teachers with the tools to facilitate a more impactful learning experience for students with disabilities. 

Parents are also typically involved and encouraged to use ABA principles at home as well to create consistent behavioral intervention between home and school. For example, if a child undergoing ABA therapy has been taught not to shout out in class any longer, his parents might be taught to reinforce this boundary at home.

Sometimes students exhibit the same problem behaviors at school as they do at home. Some students may behave worse at school and better at home. ABA can be used to address a range of behavior and give parents the tools they need to appropriately correct children who act out.

If you have questions about our behavior consultation services or have a referral, please be sure to contact us. 

Our Focus

ABA is a science dedicated to the understanding and improvement of human behavior. In that, ABA addresses all behaviors. Engaging any child or young adult in positive social interactions that teaches appropriate skills and reduces challenging behaviors are vital components of behavioral therapy.

Our Services

Our ABA services are provided by a Lead Analyst or a Board-Certified Behavior Analyst and a Registered Behavior Technician (RBT). Lead Analysts and BCBAs focus on defining behaviors of social significance, sometimes referred to target behaviors in children between 18 months and 12 years and young adults between 13 and 21 years.

Lead Analysts and BCBAs also provide coaching and support to assist those with close and continual contact with the child or young adult including school staff and administrators, health care providers, after-school or day care staff. 

Why Behavior Consultation

Behavior consultation recognizes that negative or maladaptive behaviors  can affect not only the child or young adult but also family members and social peers. ABA therapy can be used to address almost any behavior. Its principles can increase or decrease certain behaviors and improve socially-significant behaviors including social skills, expressive and receptive communication, and repetitive or stereotyped behaviors.

Kidz Companion, Inc. is an independent, certified behavioral health company offering in-home ABA therapy for children (18 months-12 years) and young adults (13-21 years) with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) and related developmental disorders in Florida.
